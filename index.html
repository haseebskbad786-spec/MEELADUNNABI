About

About the Application

Meeladunnabi Result Management System is a professional digital platform developed for managing Islamic arts and academic competitions efficiently. The system provides live result publishing, automatic point calculation, team ranking management and secure administration facilities.

About Administration

This platform is managed by the authorized Meeladunnabi Committee Administration. Only verified administrators are permitted to update programs, participants, results and team scores.
